Affiliated Clubs

AFC West End

West End Football Club changed its name to AFC West End in the recent past. The club runs successful adult and youth football teams which play in local leagues on Sundays.

  • Sunday 1st Team (Jaguars) AFC West End (Guildford & Woking Alliance Football League)
  • West End Vets (Saturday Social Football)
  • AFC West End Youth (Sunday Surrey Youth Leagues)

West End Cricket Club

West End Cricket Club plays league cricket on Saturdays and cup cricket on Sundays. The youth section run organised practise for children of all ages and play matches on Sunday mornings.

  • West End CC (2 sides) (1st & 2nd teams Fullers Brewery Surrey County League)

Other Clubs & Societies

The Pavilion (our Clubhouse) also hosts the West End Golf Club, the West End Pidgeon Club and the West End Classic Car Club.
For more information on these please enquire at the Bar.
